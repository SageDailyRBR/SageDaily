Your daily quote ritual
SageDaily greets you with today’s date, then reveals one quote chosen for the day.
The quote stays the same every time you reopen the app on that date, creating a small daily ritual.

Features:

one quote per day

same quote all day long

Italian and English language support

calm animated sky background

simple settings screen to change language anytime

fully on-device experience

Open the app, breathe, smile, and let the day begin.
